Path of Sin: Greed

Uncover the Secrets, Solve the Mystery – Justice Awaits!

Game Description

Path of Sin: Greed is an immersive detective adventure where players take on the role of an investigator solving a high-profile murder in a secluded mansion. Unravel hidden clues, interrogate suspects, and uncover shocking secrets as you piece together the mystery. With beautifully designed environments, challenging puzzles, and a gripping narrative, every decision you make influences the course of the investigation.

How to Play

  • Investigate the Crime Scene – Search for hidden objects, collect clues, and analyze evidence.

  • Solve Puzzles & Riddles – Unlock doors, decipher codes, and connect the dots to move forward.

  • Interrogate Suspects – Choose your questions wisely to expose lies and discover motives.

  • Make Critical Decisions – Your choices affect the storyline and determine the case’s outcome.

  • Explore a Dark and Twisting Narrative – Engage in an unpredictable story filled with intrigue and deception.

Main Characters

  • Detective Anna Rivers

    A sharp-minded investigator known for her keen observation skills. She is determined to uncover the truth, no matter how deeply it's buried. Her logical thinking and ability to read people make her a formidable detective.

  • Nicholas Wright

    A wealthy businessman and owner of the secluded mansion where the crime took place. He appears cooperative, but his past holds many secrets that may be connected to the murder.

  • Eleanor Graves

    A mysterious housekeeper with years of service in the mansion. She knows every hidden passage and dark secret within its walls. Her loyalty to the family may hide deeper motivations.
